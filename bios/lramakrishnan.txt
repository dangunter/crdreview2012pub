Lavanya Ramakrishnan joined LBL as an Alvarez Postdoctoral Fellow in 2009. She complete her doctoral degree from Indiana University, Bloomington in June 2009. Previously, she was a research staff member at MCNC and the Renaissance Computing Institute (RENCI) in North Carolina. Her research focus is on resource and workflow management for scientific applications in grid and cloud environments. Her master’s degree is in Computer Science from Indiana University and her bachelor’s degree is in Computer Engineering from the University of Mumbai in India.
